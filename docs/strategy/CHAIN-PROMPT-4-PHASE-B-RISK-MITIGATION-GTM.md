## CRITICAL RISK MATRIX

### RISK 1: Competitive Disruption (Mitchell 1 / Legacy Player Response)
**Probability**: High (60%) | **Impact**: Medium | **Timeline**: Months 6-12
**Mitigation**:
- First-mover AI advantage (18-month development lead)
- Customer switching costs (integration deep, workflow embedded)
- OEM partnerships lock (exclusive arrangements with 2-3 major OEMs)
- Pricing strategy: Premium (not discount) = sustainable margins

### RISK 2: Customer Adoption & Churn
**Probability**: Medium (35%) | **Impact**: High | **Timeline**: Months 3-9
**Mitigation**:
- 2-week implementation (vs. 12 weeks for competitors)
- Dedicated customer success team from Day 1
- Onboarding guarantee (ROI realization within 3 months or refund)
- Expansion playbook (Services revenue 2-3x initial SaaS MRR)

### RISK 3: Technician Adoption (Workflow Resistance)
**Probability**: Low-Medium (25%) | **Impact**: Medium | **Timeline**: Months 2-8
**Mitigation**:
- Mobile-first app design (meets technicians where they work)
- Gamification & incentive programs (technician performance bonuses)
- Union engagement (partner with technician associations for buy-in)
- "Show me the money" proof (Harley case study: +28% productivity)

### RISK 4: OEM Partnership Delays
**Probability**: Medium (40%) | **Impact**: Medium-High | **Timeline**: Months 12-24
**Mitigation**:
- Parallel OEM conversations (5-7 pilots in parallel, not sequential)
- Reference partnerships with tier-2 OEMs first (faster decision cycles)
- Warranty integration white-paper (published, third-party validated)
- Revenue model works without OEM deals (B2B repair networks primary path)

### RISK 5: Regulatory/Compliance
**Probability**: Low (10%) | **Impact**: High | **Timeline**: Variable
**Mitigation**:
- Data privacy by design (GDPR + CCPA compliant from Day 1)
- Cybersecurity framework (SOC 2 Type II audit in Year 1)
- Automotive industry standards (ISO 26262 functional safety roadmap)
- Legal budget reserved ($200K Year 1)

### RISK 6: Fundraising Constraints
**Probability**: Medium (35%) | **Impact**: High | **Timeline**: Months 3-6
**Mitigation**:
- $3M pre-seed already committed (reduces Series A urgency)
- Revenue-based financing backup (revenue-backed loans if Series A delayed)
- Profitability pathway by Q3 Year 2 (independent of future raises)
- Covenant flexibility in any funding (avoid restrictive terms)

## GO-TO-MARKET CONTINGENCY SCENARIOS

### SCENARIO A: Enterprise Sales Misses (50% of Year 1 target)
**Trigger**: 0 enterprise deals closed by Month 4
**Response**:
1. Pivot to mid-market acceleration (faster sales cycles)
2. Launch self-serve digital offering (SMB segment, lower ACV)
3. Accelerate marketplace revenue (API licensing to third-party tools)
4. Adjusted Year 1 target: $4.5M (still profitable by Year 3)

### SCENARIO B: Competitive Price War
**Trigger**: Legacy player drops pricing 30-40% below RideWire
**Response**:
1. Hold premium positioning (emphasize AI quality, not price)
2. Increase Service revenue focus (3x margin advantage vs. SaaS discounts)
3. Lock customers with multi-year agreements (lock-in value)
4. Expand into OEM channel (higher ARPU, less price sensitive)

### SCENARIO C: OEM Partnership Fails
**Trigger**: 0 OEM partnerships by Month 12
**Response**:
1. Accelerate B2B repair network expansion (still $450M SAM)
2. Focus on marketplace monetization (API licensing revenue)
3. Pursue regional distributor model (less direct channel)
4. Maintain profitability path without OEM revenue
