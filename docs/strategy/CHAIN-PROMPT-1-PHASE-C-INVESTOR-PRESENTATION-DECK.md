✅ **Visuals over Text**: Charts, live demos, real customer data
✅ **Emotional + Rational**: Problem hook + unit economics proof

✅ **Credibility**: Pilot results, team experience, market validation
✅ **Simplicity**: 12 slides, 10-15 minutes, no jargon
✅ **Data-Driven**: Every claim has a number or customer proof
