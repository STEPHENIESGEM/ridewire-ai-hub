**Actions**:
- [ ] Open RideWire app on tablet

#### Step 2: Basic Component Recognition (1.5 minutes)
**Actions**:

## ‚ö†Ô∏è TROUBLESHOOTING DURING DEMO

### AR Overlay Not Appearing
**Causes**:
- Camera permissions not granted
- Poor lighting conditions
- Motorcycle too far from camera
- App needs restart

**Fix on the Fly**:
1. Check camera permissions in settings
2. Move motorcycle to better lighting
3. Move closer to motorcycle (3-6 feet ideal)
4. Restart app if needed
5. Use backup device if persistent
6. Switch to showing pre-recorded AR video

---

### Component Misidentification
**If AR labels wrong component**:
- Acknowledge: "That's interesting - it's still learning"
- Correct: Manually select correct component
- Explain: "System improves as it sees more examples"
- Positive spin: "This is exactly the feedback we need from testing"

---

### Laggy or Slow Rendering
**If AR overlay is choppy**:
- Close background apps to free memory
- Reduce number of components shown simultaneously
- Move to area with better WiFi signal
- Explain: "This is beta - optimization is ongoing"
- Use screenshots from smooth-running demo

---

### Can't Connect to Motorcycle
**If live diagnostics won't connect**:
- Switch to simulation mode
- Use pre-recorded examples
- Explain: "The concept is what matters today"
- Schedule full integration demo for Test Phase 2
