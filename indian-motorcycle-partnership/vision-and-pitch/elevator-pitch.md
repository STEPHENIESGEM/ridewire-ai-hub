## üé¨ DELIVERY TIPS

### Opening (15 seconds)
- **Start with a compelling visual**: "Imagine..."
- **Create a vivid scene**: Technician facing diagnostic challenge
- **Hook their attention**: Something they can picture immediately

### Body (90 seconds)
- **Explain the solution clearly**: Multi-AI + AR
- **Use simple language**: Avoid excessive jargon
- **Focus on benefits**: What's in it for them
- **Include proof points**: Real metrics and capabilities
- **Show credibility**: Production-ready, enterprise-grade

### Close (15 seconds)
- **Clear call to action**: Partnership invitation
- **Low-risk proposition**: No cost testing
- **Appeal to values**: Innovation, leadership
- **Invite response**: "What do you think?" or "Ready to lead?"

## üí° ADAPTATION GUIDE

### Adjust Based on Audience Response

#### If They Seem Excited
- **Go deeper**: Offer demo immediately
- **Discuss timeline**: "We could start testing next week"
- **Talk specifics**: "Which of your technicians should be involved?"
- **Capture momentum**: "Let's schedule the detailed session"

#### If They Seem Skeptical
- **Acknowledge concerns**: "I understand the hesitation"
- **Offer proof**: "Let me show you it actually working"
- **Reduce perceived risk**: "Zero commitment, just exploration"
- **Find their pain point**: "What's your biggest diagnostic challenge?"

#### If They Have Questions
- **Welcome them**: "Great question, let me address that"
- **Be honest**: Don't oversell or exaggerate
- **Provide details**: Have technical answers ready
- **Show flexibility**: "We can customize for your needs"
